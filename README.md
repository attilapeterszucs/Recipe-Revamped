## 🌟 **About RecipeRevamped**

RecipeRevamped is the ultimate AI-powered SaaS platform that revolutionizes how people approach cooking and dietary restrictions. We transform your favorite recipes to perfectly match any dietary preference or health goal, making delicious, personalized meals accessible to everyone.

### **🎯 Our Mission**

To make healthy, personalized cooking effortless for everyone by leveraging cutting-edge AI technology to adapt any recipe to any dietary need, instantly.

## 🍽️ **What We Do**

### **AI Recipe Transformation**
Our advanced AI engine, powered by OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini, intelligently converts any recipe to match your specific dietary requirements:

- **🌱 Vegan & Vegetarian** - Plant-based alternatives that taste amazing
- **🚫 Gluten-Free** - Safe, delicious options for celiac and gluten sensitivity
- **🥩 Keto & Low-Carb** - High-fat, low-carb adaptations for ketogenic lifestyles
- **🥜 Allergy-Friendly** - Nut-free, dairy-free, and other allergen-safe versions
- **💪 High-Protein** - Muscle-building modifications for fitness enthusiasts
- **🍯 Sugar-Free** - Healthy sweetener alternatives for diabetics
- **🌾 Paleo** - Primitive diet adaptations using whole foods

### **Smart Meal Planning**
- **📅 Calendar Integration** - Plan your weeks with converted recipes
- **🛒 Shopping Lists** - Auto-generated ingredient lists with substitutions
- **📊 Nutritional Analysis** - Detailed macro and micronutrient breakdowns
- **⏰ Prep Time Optimization** - Efficient cooking schedules and meal prep guides

### **Premium Features**
- **📱 Mobile-Optimized** - Cook anywhere with our responsive design
- **💾 Recipe Collection** - Save and organize your favorite adaptations
- **🔄 Backup & Sync** - Never lose your recipes across devices
- **📤 Export Options** - PDF and print-friendly formats
- **👥 Family Sharing** - Share meal plans with family members
